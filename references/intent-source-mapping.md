# MayGrove AI QA: Intent-to-Source Mapping

This reference documents how the 7 intent categories map to the available knowledge sources, what each category needs, and how to handle gaps.

## 7 Intent Categories

### PlantingTech（种植技术）
- **Definition**: Technical plant-growing questions: watering, lighting, nutrients, seedlings, transplanting, climbing systems, root management, pruning, pests, harvesting.
- **Primary sources**: `MayGrove_Verti_30__V30__使用说明书.md` §7–§15.
- **Well-covered topics**: EC/pH-based dosing, tank cleaning, pump self-check, climbing-plant pod placement, seedling box use, root pruning.
- **Gap topics**: Specific pest/disease identification, plant-variety-specific care, harvest timing per plant.
- **Gap handling**: Use web research with authoritative gardening sites (`thespruce.com`, `costafarms.com`, `extension.org`).

### ProductInquiry（产品咨询）
- **Definition**: Device and app questions: specs, unboxing, installation, accessories, power, connectivity, troubleshooting, warranty.
- **Primary sources**: V30 manual §1, §3, §5, §6, §8, §13, §17–§19; `Maygrove.md` device binding and account sections.
- **Well-covered topics**: Tank capacity, dimensions, weight, power, Wi-Fi, self-check, replacement parts, safety warnings.
- **Gap topics**: Pricing, purchase channels, warranty claim process details, region-specific certifications.
- **Gap handling**: Avoid generating QA for price/purchase unless a dedicated source is provided. Route warranty questions to manual only.

### GrowingPlan（种植规划）
- **Definition**: App onboarding flow, profile fields, plan generation, plan entry points, plan actions.
- **Primary sources**: `Maygrove.md` 种植规划 / 个人中心 Plan / 种子库维护.
- **Well-covered topics**: Flow steps, profile questions, entry points, buttons, default device capacity (30+2).
- **Gap topics**: Botanical recommendations generated by the AI plan, seasonal planting calendars.
- **Gap handling**: Keep QA focused on the App flow. Botany recommendations should be classified under PlantingTech or Lifestyle.

### Lifestyle（健康有趣的生活）
- **Definition**: Home décor, gifting, family activities, community vibe, low-risk plant recommendations for living spaces.
- **Primary sources**: V30 manual intro (kitchen/living-room placement), `Maygrove.md` 推荐问 lifestyle examples, web research.
- **Well-covered topics**: Where to place the device, general gift/kids prompts from the app.
- **Gap topics**: Concrete plant suggestions, Feng Shui or health claims, pet-safe plant lists.
- **Gap handling**: Use web research; phrase recommendations as "commonly suggested" or "popular choices". Never claim health benefits.

### ChatEmotional（闲聊情感类）
- **Definition**: Greetings, emotional support, small talk, encouragement, persona tone.
- **Primary sources**: `Maygrove.md` 日常招呼区 / 自由问答.
- **Well-covered topics**: Fixed first-login greeting, random greeting library, persona name (Planting Bestie / Gardener in Your Kitchen), tone requirements.
- **Gap topics**: Full copy library, edge-case emotional responses, escalation paths.
- **Gap handling**: Generate QA for documented greetings and tone. Mark broader emotional responses as needing a curated persona library.

### DietCooking（饮食健康与烹饪食谱）
- **Definition**: Using harvested ingredients in recipes, food safety, simple kitchen ideas.
- **Primary sources**: V30 manual §15.12 (food safety), `Maygrove.md` 推荐问 recipe examples, web research.
- **Well-covered topics**: Food-safety disclaimer, pet-toxicity note, recipe inspiration prompts.
- **Gap topics**: Actual recipes, ingredient ratios, nutritional claims.
- **Gap handling**: Always use web research for recipes. Include the V30 manual safety disclaimer and pet-toxicity note in every answer.

### OutofScope（超出范围）
- **Definition**: Anything unrelated to MayGrove, plants, or the app.
- **Primary sources**: Brand-boundary definition.
- **Well-covered topics**: What to refuse (politics, finance, medical diagnosis, coding, PII).
- **Gap handling**: None. Generate clearly off-topic questions and deflection answers. The answer must not answer the original question.

## Default Source Priority

1. Local V30 manual
2. Local Maygrove.md app documentation
3. Web-research facts (for Lifestyle, DietCooking, and PlantingTech gaps)
4. Brand policy (for OutofScope)
